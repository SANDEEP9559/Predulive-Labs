Spectra-90

  1. Home
  2. Spectra-90

Harness the Power of Hybrid VTOL Technology for Unparalleled Aerial Performance!

Spectra-90 stands out as a Hybrid VTOL Drone incorporating advanced AI technology, making it an invaluable asset for energy infrastructure inspection. Beyond safety enhancements and data collection prowess, this drone significantly bolsters the reliability of global energy infrastructure. Its capabilities extend to large-scale mapping, offering comprehensive solutions for various industries.

By leveraging AI, Spectra-90 not only improves operational efficiency but also ensures thorough and accurate inspections, contributing to safer and more reliable energy systems worldwide. Its versatility and cutting-edge features position it as a key player in enhancing infrastructure management, paving the way for smarter and more sustainable practices.

Specifications of Spectra-90

Versatile Operations: No need for a runway or launchpad.

Flight Endurance: 90 Min.

Payload Capacity: 2 kg
